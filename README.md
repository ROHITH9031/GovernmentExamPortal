# 📚 Exam Guidance Hub

> Your Complete Guide to Government Examinations in India

A comprehensive, full-stack web portal designed to help aspirants navigate and prepare for Central & State Government examinations. Built with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

**Live Demo:** [Exam Guidance Hub](https://john-wesleyS.github.io/FSD-Project/)

---

## 🎯 Overview

**Exam Guidance Hub** is a centralized platform offering:
- **Latest Job Postings** (Central & State Government)
- **Exam Notifications & Alerts** (UPSC, SSC, RRB, Banking, etc.)
- **Complete Syllabi & Exam Patterns**
- **Previous Year Question Papers** with Solutions
- **Cut-off Marks** Analysis
- **Admit Cards & Results** Portal
- **Mock Tests** & Practice Materials
- **Study Resources** (Notes, Books, Videos)
- **Exam Calendar** with Important Dates
- **Answer Keys** (Official & Provisional)

### 📊 By The Numbers
- **500+** Government Job Postings
- **200+** Active Notifications
- **1000+** Question Papers
- **50+** Complete Syllabi
- **100+** Mock Tests
- **100% FREE** - No Hidden Charges

---

## ✨ Key Features

### 🔍 Job Search & Filtering
- Filter jobs by **level** (Central/State)
- Filter by **qualification** requirements
- Search by **department** or **post name**
- Real-time updates on vacancies

### 📋 Comprehensive Exam Information
- **Syllabus Details** - Topic-wise breakdown
- **Exam Patterns** - Marking schemes & structure
- **Cut-off Analysis** - Category-wise previous year marks
- **Exam Calendar** - Dates in calendar & timeline views

### 📚 Study Materials
- **Free Question Papers** - Download PDFs
- **Study Notes** - Subject-wise organized notes
- **Recommended Books** - Best resources for each exam
- **Video Lectures** - Curated learning content
- **Current Affairs** - Monthly digests

### 🎯 Test & Practice
- **Full-Length Mock Tests** - Timed, scored, analyzed
- **Sectional Tests** - Section-wise practice
- **Topic-wise Tests** - Master specific topics
- **Instant Results** - Detailed performance analysis

### 🔔 Notifications & Updates
- **Admit Card Downloads** - Track release dates
- **Result Announcements** - Latest exam results
- **Application Deadlines** - Never miss a deadline
- **Important Alerts** - Urgent notifications highlighted
