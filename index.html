Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about government exams

What is the age limit for government exams?

+

The age limit varies. Most UPSC exams require candidates to be between 21-32 years. State level exams may differ. Always check the official notification.

How to prepare for SSC exams?

+

Understand the exam pattern and syllabus. Practice previous year papers, join coaching or online courses, and take regular mock tests.

What documents are required for examination?

+

Generally: valid photo ID (Aadhar, PAN, Passport), admit card, and any documents mentioned in the official notification.

How to download question papers?

+

Visit our Question Papers section, select exam and year, and click download. PDFs are available for all major exams.
